Frequently Asked Questions

What is FusionAI?
FusionAI is a professional multi-agent platform that combines multiple AI models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) with specialized tools for web search, document creation, social media analysis, and more. It's designed to be your all-in-one AI workspace.
How do I use different AI agents?
Click the "Multi-Bot Search" button in the top-left corner of the chat interface. This opens the agent drawer where you can select from 16+ specialized agents including Multi-Bot Search, Custom Trainer, Web Search, Reddit Explorer, and more.
Can I upload files for analysis?
Yes! Click the paperclip icon next to the input field to upload images, PDFs, Word documents, Excel files, text files, and more. The AI will analyze the content and provide insights based on your instructions.
How does voice input work?
Click the microphone button next to the input field to start voice recording. Speak naturally, and the system will automatically convert your speech to text. It will stop after a pause and send the message automatically.
What are Connected Apps?
Connected Apps allow FusionAI to integrate with external tools like Figma, Canva, Notion, and n8n. You can manage these connections in Settings. Connected apps show a green dot and can be used directly from the chat interface.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely! All conversations are encrypted and private. You're currently in guest mode, but you can create an account to save your history across devices. We never share your data with third parties.
How do I change the theme?
Use the theme toggle buttons (Light/Dark) in the top-right corner of any page. Your preference is saved locally and will persist across sessions.
